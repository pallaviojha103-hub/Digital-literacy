           Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist


1. Never share your OTP (One-Time Password), UPI PIN, bank details, or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.

2. Do not accept payment requests from unknown or unverified people on UPI apps.

3. Always double-check before scanning any QR code, as scanning a QR code can send money instead of receiving it.

4. Do not click on suspicious or unknown links received through SMS, email, or social media platforms.

5. Use only official and trusted payment applications such as Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm.

6. Always verify the identity of the person before making any online transaction.

7. Enable SMS and app notifications to keep track of every transaction happening in your account.

8. Use strong and unique passwords for your accounts and change them regularly.

9. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible for extra security.

10. Avoid using public Wi-Fi networks while making financial transactions.

11. Keep your mobile phone, apps, and operating system updated to prevent security vulnerabilities.

12. Be cautious of calls or messages that create urgency or fear, as fraudsters often use these tactics to manipulate victims.

13. Do not save sensitive information like passwords or PINs on your phone in plain text.

14. Immediately report any suspicious activity or fraud on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in.

15. Call the Cyber Crime Helpline Number 1930 as soon as possible in case of financial fraud to increase the chances of recovering your money.
