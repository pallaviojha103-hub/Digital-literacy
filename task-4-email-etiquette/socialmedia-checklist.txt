                       
               Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College students






Do's:-

1. Use strong and secure passwords for all your accounts.

2. Think before posting anything online.

3. Maintain a professional and respectful profile.

4. Share useful and positive content.

5. Respect others and communicate politely.

6. Use privacy settings to protect your personal information.

7. Verify information before sharing it.
 

Don'ts:-

1. Do not share personal details like phone number, address, or passwords.

2. Do not post inappropriate or offensive content.

3. Do not engage in cyberbullying or online fights.

4. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.

5. Do not spread fake news or rumors.

6. Do not use weak passwords.

7. Do not overshare your personal life or location.
